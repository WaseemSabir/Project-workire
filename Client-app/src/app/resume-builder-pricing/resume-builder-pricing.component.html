What are AI Credits?

Workire AI Writer is powered by GPT-3 API from OpenAI. This means, with every bullet point you generate, we're "buying" the usage of their AI technology. Every request you make costs Workire money. Workire Pro's 100,000 including AI credits are approximately equal to 400 unique bullet points, or 9 full resumes.

What is Workire Lifetime?

Workire Lifetime is the most popular Workire plan which gives you access to all premium Workire features at a one-time cost rather than a monthly subscription. Compared to Workire Pro, Workire Lifetime does not include free resume reviews or 100,000 monthly AI Credits.

Is Workire Lifetime the same as Workire Pro?

Almost. Workire Lifetime does not include free monthly reviews or 100,000 monthly AI Credits.

What is a Workire Resume Review?

A Workire Expert can help you polish your resume into exactly what recruiters are looking for. We'll correct all formatting, content, and grammar errors directly in your resume. It is only possible for us to review resumes created in Workire. We do not offer reviews for non-Workire resumes.

What is your refund policy?

You may request a refund for your current month if you request it within 24 hours of the transaction. We reserve the right to decline that request should you use our software within this time. Workire Lifetime is not refundable.

Where can I upgrade my plan?

Workire Account Billing - First you'll need to create a free Workire account, next you can navigate to the billing page within your dashboard
